About Us

Quick Dial · cilacila.cn

Our Mission

Quick Dial is a minimalist browser new tab tool designed for desktop users, dedicated to providing a clean, ad-free, and efficient browser start page experience. We offer multi-engine search, website navigation, and practical utilities with no popups or forced notifications.

Service Policy

All features are free services. We do not collect private data, force redirects, bundle software, or hijack traffic. All searches redirect to official engine results pages.

Our Philosophy

We reject cluttered feeds and advertising. Clean, minimalist, and efficient design for a pure browsing experience.
