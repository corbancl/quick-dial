Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 4, 2026

Quick Dial (cilacila.cn) highly values user privacy protection. This Privacy Policy explains our information collection, usage, and protection practices.

1. Information Collection

We do not actively collect, store, or upload any personal privacy information, including but not limited to phone numbers, account passwords, geolocation, browsing history, or device information. We do not use any analytics tools, tracking scripts, or behavioral monitoring. All user data remains strictly local to the browser.

2. Search Data

Our search functionality only provides a redirect entry point. All user search behavior, search content, and search results are completed on third-party official search engine pages. We do not save, capture, or record user search keywords or browsing history.

3. Cookie Usage

We only use minimal local Cookies to save user personalization settings (such as theme colors, search engine preferences, and widget toggle settings). All Cookies are stored only in the user's local browser, are not uploaded to servers, and do not collect any private information.

4. Third-Party Services

Some features rely on legitimate third-party public services (search engines, weather APIs, site analytics). All third-party services comply with their respective platform privacy rules. We strictly screen compliant third-party interfaces to ensure user access security.

5. User Rights

Users may at any time clear local cache, Cookies, and local configuration data from this site through their browser settings, maintaining full control over personal browsing data.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed for operational purposes. Updates will be publicly posted on this page and take effect upon posting.
