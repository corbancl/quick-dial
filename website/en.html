FAQ

Is the free version enough?
Absolutely. 6 engines, 3 groups, all wallpapers, and weather for free. Pro adds cloud sync, more engines, unlimited groups — ideal for multi-device users.
Do you collect my data?
No. All data is stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage. Cloud sync is manually triggered, data is encrypted in transit, and we cannot read your dials.
Forgot password?
Bind your email after logging in, then reset your password on the website. Go to Account →
Works on mobile?
Yes. The web version works on mobile and can be set as your browser homepage. Chrome mobile doesn't support extensions yet.
