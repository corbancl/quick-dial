FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free version enough?
Absolutely. The free version includes 6 search engines, 3 navigation groups, all wallpaper presets and weather. Pro adds cloud sync, more engines, and unlimited groups for multi-device users.
Do you collect my data?
No. All data is stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage. Cloud sync is triggered by you, data is encrypted in transit, and we cannot view your bookmarks.
Forgot your password?
Bind your email after login to recover your password anytime. Go to Account →
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. The web version is mobile-optimized and can be set as your phone browser's homepage. Chrome for Android currently doesn't support extensions.
Can I get a refund for Pro?
Yes. Unconditional refund within 7 days of purchase. Contact support.
